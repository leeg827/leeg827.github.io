The damp path zigzagged like those of my childhood. When we reached the house, we went into a library filled with books from both East and West.I recognized some large volumes bound in yellow silk-manuscripts of the Lost Encyclopedia which was edited by the Third Emperor of the Luminous Dynasty. They had never been printed.
