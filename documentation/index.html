ON BEGINNINGS by kristy hyun explores the themes of the poem "On Beginnings" From Madness, Rack, and Honey by Mary Ruefle through the medium of the web. Her use of css animation, typography, and a minimalist color palette creates a unique navigation experience for the user.

Design elements are very minimalist in order to focus on the text of the website.
